About Green Roots

Who we are, what we stand for, and why it matters.

Our Mission

Green Roots is a community led project that is a combination of local reforestation with utilising and teaching actual digital skills around the real world. We believe that protecting the environment and growing the next generation of developers are goals that go together and should combine to something greater like us.

Founded 13th June 2026, we're in our very first chapter and that means every person who joins now is a founder, our volunteers don't just plant trees... they build the digital tools we use to track, promote, and expand our work.

Our values

The principles that guide every decision we make.

Environmental responsibility

Every action we take is guided by its long term impact on the ecosystems.

Inclusivity

We welcome volunteers of all backgrounds, skill levels, and abilities.

Learning by doing

Real world experience beats theory working. Our volunteers develop skills through proper project work.

Transparency

We publish our planting data and project progress openly so anyone can see our impact.
