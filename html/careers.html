Junior data analyst

Part-time

Up to 10 hrs/week  |  Remote / Wales

Help us track and visualise the environmental impact of our planting projects. You'll work with planting data, build simple dashboards, and help the team understand what's working and what's not. Great experience for anyone looking to grow their computing or data skills in a real project setting.

Required skills & experience

  • Basic HTML, CSS & JavaScript
  • Familiarity with Excel or Google Sheets
  • Ability to present data clearly

Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in or completed Level 3 Computing (BTEC or A-Level)
  • No industry certifications required — enthusiasm counts
